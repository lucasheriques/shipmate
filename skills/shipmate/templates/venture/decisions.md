# Decision journal

> Append-only bet journal. "Pass bar" and "expected" are written **before** data exists; "actual" and "learning" are filled at the weekly retro, never at bet time. A founder arguing with the bar after seeing data is the signal the bar was doing its job.
>
> Tag entries `#framework` when the shipmate pipeline itself misrouted you (wrong stage diagnosis, exit test passed a stage that later collapsed, kill rule fired too early/late) — and consider filing it as an issue on the shipmate repo so the framework learns your scar.
